The component uses the `useScroll` hook by framer motion. In order to achieve a nice effect, you likely have to play with the container (where to track the scroll) and its offset. Please refer to framer's [documentation](https://www.framer.com/motion/use-scroll/) for more details.

Make sure that the container has a non-static position, like `relative`, `fixed`, or `absolute` to ensure scroll offset is calculated correctly.
